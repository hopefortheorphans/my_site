About Our Charity

Hope for the Orphans was founded in 2023 as a community-Based Non-Government Organization operating in Uganda, Busiita sub-county, Sironko district. It started as a family Foundation with 8 kids from remote families. Currently, we are having 80 kids.

The main purpose of the proposed project is to provide orphans and vulnerable children with adequate accommodation and living conditions. As a childcare/Orphanage, we do provide a home for our children basing on the child’s status or condition. He or she is initiated in our family setting from where they find a sense of belonging with our mother who happens to be a widow and able to take care of the child together with other siblings in a home. We do emphasize our children being molded and nurtured under a family institute as this is an avenue for which right and proper morals of life are instilled.

Currently, we rent two homestead which accommodates 80 children, with loving mothers providing good care to the children. Due to the expansion of our vision and the need to increase the number of orphans in our care with the high costs of renting a house, we are planning to construct a home in Busiita - Sironko District, access to enough space is a basic requirement of a childcare center. This purpose will be achieved through the mobilization of financial resources from local entrepreneurs, and global donors, and the construction of a new orphanage building. The project is scheduled to start in January 2025 and be completed by the end of September 2027

Popular Causes

We are a non-profital & Charity raising money for child education

Education

We work with children living in rural and urban extreme poverty to improve their access to a quality education, particularly focusing on marginalised groups including girls, orphans and children with disabilities.

Health

All Children, wheather he/she is staying with HIV/AIDS, Affected by parental ill health, or having recurring medical problems, should be able to accesss healthcare. Our projects support marginalised children to get the help they need to stay as physically and mentally healthy as possibe.

Sustainable Livelihood

The best place for a child to grow up is within their loving family. Parents utilise their resourcefulness and determination through income generation schemes to create stable economic future and a better life for their children. Increasing household health will mean that parents no longer rely on their children to support the family income through working, and can afford school costs.
