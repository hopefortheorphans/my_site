Education

We work with children living in rural and urban extreme poverty to improve their access to a quality education, particularly focusing on marginalised groups including girls, orphans and children with disabilities.

Health

All Children, wheather he/she is staying with HIV/AIDS, Affected by parental ill health, or having recurring medical problems, should be able to accesss healthcare. Our projects support marginalised children to get the help they need to stay as physically and mentally healthy as possibe.

Sustainable Livelihood

The best place for a child to grow up is within their loving family. Parents utilise their resourcefulness and determination through income generation schemes to create stable economic future and a better life for their children. Increasing household health will mean that parents no longer rely on their children to support the family income through working, and can afford school costs.
